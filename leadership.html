The Leadership Of Our Firm

Defining Ndlela Kunene.

Ndlela Kunene Pty Ltd ("Ndlela Kunene" / "firm") is a Johannesburg-based technology consulting firm that provides cost-effective solutions to both the public and private sectors. We specialise in IT governance, technology risk advisory, information security, digital forensics, data analytics and IT audit. Our services and advice support external and internal audit, risk advisors and act as independent consultants for boards, executive management, IT management, and business unit managers.

As a growing cybersecurity consulting, audit and advisory firm, Ndlela Kunene is exceptionally positioned to provide quality, cost-efficient, and top-class expert solutions to clients that include government, national corporations, non-profits, finance, manufacturing, education, technology, media, retail, healthcare, mining and energy.

Our Cyber Risk & Governance services practice can help protect your organisation's information systems, ensure compliance with regulatory requirements, and provide insights to leverage IT controls to reduce your costs and gain competitive advantage.

We pride ourselves on clear and methodical practices based on leading frameworks, and a comprehensive range of skills to meet the most demanding requirements.

Our head office is located in Johannesburg, but we have the capacity, availability, presence and network to service clients anywhere within Southern Africa with minimal logistical challenges.

Memberships.

  • The Institute of IT Professionals South African (IITPSA)

    South Africa's professional body for IT practitioners, promoting ethical standards and professional development.

  • Institute of Internal Auditors (IIA)
